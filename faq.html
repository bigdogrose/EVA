General Questions

  • What is EVA?
    EVA is an advanced verbal assistant designed to provide companionship and support for individuals living with dementia. It engages in conversations, offers memory support, and enhances the overall quality of life for users.
  • How does EVA work?
    EVA uses natural language processing to communicate with users. It can engage in conversations, provide reminders, share interactive stories, and offer emotional support.
  • Is EVA easy to use?
    Yes, EVA is designed to be user-friendly. It can be customized to meet individual needs and preferences, making it accessible to a wide range of users.

Technical Questions

  • What devices are compatible with EVA?
    EVA is compatible with smartphones and tablets running Android and iOS. You can download the EVA app from the respective app stores.
  • Is EVA available in multiple languages?
    Yes, EVA supports multiple languages, making it accessible to a global audience.
  • Is there a cost for using EVA?
    EVA offers both free and premium subscription plans. The premium plan unlocks all features and provides additional benefits.

Privacy and Security

  • Is my data secure with EVA?
    We take your privacy seriously. EVA is designed with strong security measures to protect user data and maintain confidentiality.
  • Does EVA store recordings of conversations?
    EVA does not store or share recordings of user conversations. Privacy and confidentiality are a top priority.

Getting Started

  • How do I get started with EVA?
    Getting started with EVA is simple. Download the app, create an account, and follow the setup instructions to customize EVA for your needs.
  • Can EVA be used by multiple family members?
    Yes, EVA offers a family plan that allows multiple family members to connect and use the service together.
  • What if I have technical issues or questions?
    We offer customer support to assist with technical issues and answer any questions you may have. Contact our support team for help.
