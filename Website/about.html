Your Trusted Companion

EVA is a groundbreaking verbal assistant designed to provide companionship, support, and enhance the quality of life for individuals living with dementia.

Key Features:

  • Natural Language Processing: EVA engages in conversations, provides reminders, and offers comfort using advanced natural language processing.
  • Customizable: EVA can be tailored to individual preferences and needs, creating a personalized and enjoyable experience.
  • Memory Support: EVA helps users recall important events, names, and cherished memories.
  • Interactive Stories: EVA shares captivating stories, fostering engagement and enjoyment.
  • Family Connectivity: EVA keeps families connected through voice and text messages.
  • 24/7 Companionship: EVA is always available, offering companionship and emotional support day and night.

EVA is not just a tool; it's a trusted friend that enriches the lives of individuals with dementia and their families. Experience the difference today.

Our Mission

At EVA, we are dedicated to improving the lives of those facing the challenges of dementia. Our mission is to:

  • Provide companionship and emotional support
  • Enhance quality of life through engaging conversations and activities
  • Help individuals with dementia stay connected with their families

We believe that technology can play a vital role in offering care and support to those in need. Join us in making a positive impact on the lives of your loved ones.
